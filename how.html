How DoBid Works

DoBid seeks to empower citizens of a country

to engage in financial based political influence.

Today, only a small percentage of the population has access to finance-based political influence.

This power is not shared by all citizens, who share disproportionately less influence - to the detriment of democracy.

With DoBid, we can help by...

Social Media

With social media, there will be common interests among people.

Contracts

People who found common causes and interests can be drafted into contracts.

Acceptance

Money is then crowdfunded behind these contracts from interested person/parties. Money will be recieved bt designated recipeints if terms met.

This project closely mirrors and replicates the system used by current special interst and lobbyists.
